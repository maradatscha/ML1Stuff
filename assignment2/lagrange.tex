\section*{Constrained Optimization}

need to solve: 
\begin{eqnarray}
    \min_{\substack{x_1,x_2}}&& x_1 + 2x_2^2\\
    sb.t. && 2x_1 + x_2 = 1
\end{eqnarray}

Lagrangian: $L(x_1,x_2,\lambda) = x_1 + 2x_2^2 + \lambda(2x_1 + x_2 - 1)$

Conditions:  
\begin{eqnarray}
    \nabla_{\bf{x}} L(x_1,x_2,\lambda) &=& 0 \\
    2x_1 + x_2 - 1 &=& 0 \\
\end{eqnarray}

Solving gives us: ${\bf x} = (9/16, -1/8), \lambda = 1/2 $
